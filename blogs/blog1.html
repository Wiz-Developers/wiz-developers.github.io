BMI CALCULATOR APPLICATION

BMI or Body Mass Index is one of the most well-known methods of measuring obesity. The result of BMI is a number that determines the possibility of health risk based on your body height and weight.

The BMI formula is: $$ BMI = {weight (kg) \over height^2 (m^2)}.$$

HOW TO USE THE MOBILE APPLICATION

Download the BMI Calculator App made by Wiz Developers from PlayStore.

Select the gender from given buttons and further set the weight and height through the provided User Interface(UI) in the beautiful mobile application created by WizDevelopers with the help of flutter and Android Studio like technology .

Now, click on "Calulate" Button. This will then show BMI and status of BMI according to the scale.

WHY TO KEEP CHECK OF BMI ? The BMI calculator application serves as a precise tool for most populations to detect malnutrition, healthy weight, overweight or obesity. This allows you to avoid possible health related problems resulting from your weight. For standard BMI categories for adults, see the table below.

Range Status Risk
Less Than 18.5 Underweight Moderate-High
18.5-29.4 Normal NONE
25.0-29.9 Overweightt Moderate
More Than 29.9 Obese High
Though, BMI results are not quite accurate because they are generally valid data of an average person. BMI can be distorted for children, young people under 18, pregnant women,elderly and athletes.
